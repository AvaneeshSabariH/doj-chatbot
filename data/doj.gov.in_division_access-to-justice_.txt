Access to Justice Division implements a Central Sector Scheme to further the cause of access to justice named“Designing Innovative Solutions for Holistic Access to Justice in India (DISHA)”.The component schemes under DISHA include:-  Tele-law: Reaching the unreached, Nyaya Bandhu (Pro-bono Legal Services), Nyaya Mitra and Legal Literacy & Legal Awareness Programme.
